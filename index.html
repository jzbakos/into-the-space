MEDITATION EXPERIENCES



Guided Meditation

Change your mind, change your life. Our Guided Meditation experience will enhance your meditation practice whether you are new to meditation or an experienced meditator. Guided meditation allows you to become more mindful and aware of your feelings and thoughts, working to change your life from the inside out. Our Guided Meditation experience includes direct hypnotic suggestion; Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP) techniques; visualization; mindfulness; deep relaxation; presencing; the use of Oracle card decks. You will be encouraged to set meditation goals and share your experiences in a safe space - in a place where you are being heard and can speak your truth.



Mindful Coaching and Meditation for Businesses

This practice of mindful coaching teaches how to use mindfulness and meditation to achieve a more self-empowered/entrepreneurial and authentic approach to building your business. Suitable for individuals and teams in small and large businesses.



Sound Immersion Healing and Meditation

A sound-bath immersive guided meditation experience using the sweet, soothing sounds of our chimes, crystal and singing bowls. An evening of self-care and nurturing guiding you into a deep restorative relaxed state of mind and body. Suitable for couples, women’s circles and families.



New and Full Moon Meditation

An uplifting celebration of the moon cycles. Enjoy guided meditation experiences as we set powerful intentions with the New Moon. Release and let go of the old with the Full Moon. While immersed in a soothing deep relaxation through guided meditation. The seasonal practices of the equinox and the solstices. When possible, this is a meditation in the garden experience, under the moon and the stars, and around a fire.
